\section{Limitations}
Evidence remains mechanism-level and mostly synthetic by design. SessionKV-DR is intentionally targeted to the ALC claim, but it is not a broad downstream language benchmark. TinyQA is still lightweight and semi-realistic rather than a large public QA suite. We also observe that no-write can remain competitive on some settings, indicating unresolved write-policy/interference trade-offs. Therefore we do not claim lifelong-learning or universal long-context improvements; we claim improved delayed session-specific recall and adaptive overwrite/persistence behavior within this controlled setup.
