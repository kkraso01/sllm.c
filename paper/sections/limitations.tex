\section{Limitations}
Evidence is still primarily mechanism-level and synthetic/semi-synthetic. The language-shaped benchmark is closer to LM-style prompting than pure probes, but it is not a standard public NLP benchmark. Persistence and efficiency are currently reported from a single reference run rather than full multi-seed analysis. We also do not yet compare against stronger external-memory retrieval systems or broader long-context baselines. Finally, the competitiveness of the no-write control suggests unresolved write interference and indicates that current gains are conditional, not universal.
