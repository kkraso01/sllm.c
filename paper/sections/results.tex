\subsection{Interpretation}
ALC improves adaptation metrics on the delayed-recall task and remains stable under repeated updates. Persistence is exact in this setup. Interface optimization reduces supervised retrieval loss, supporting the utility of learnable read/fusion mappings. Runtime overhead is measurable but moderate in the tested configuration.

\subsection{Ablations}
Ablation results over slot count, update rate, routing mode, and fusion mode are exported to \texttt{paper/tables/ablation\_results.csv}. The best observed setting in this sweep is top-k softmax with additive fusion at 8 slots and update rate 0.5.
