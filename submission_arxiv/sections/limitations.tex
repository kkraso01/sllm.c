\section{Limitations}
The evaluation remains controlled and mechanism-focused. SessionKV-DR is intentionally aligned to ALC's intended function, so results should not be interpreted as broad downstream superiority. TinyQA is lightweight and supportive, not comprehensive.

No-write competitiveness on some slices indicates unresolved trade-offs in write policy and interference management. We therefore avoid claims about lifelong learning, universal long-context gains, or dominance over external retrieval and parameter-update adaptation.

Future work should extend to larger public tasks, broader model scales, and stronger cross-method baselines while preserving the same claims discipline.
