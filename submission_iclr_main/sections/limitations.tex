\section{Limitations}
This paper does not establish broad benchmark superiority. The strongest conclusions are limited to controlled delayed-recall behavior in SessionKV-DR plus lightweight semi-realistic support from TinyQA.

We therefore avoid claims about lifelong learning, universal long-context improvements, or across-the-board gains over retrieval and weight-update adaptation. Main-track-strength follow-up should include larger public benchmarks, stronger external baselines, and deeper scaling/stability analysis.
