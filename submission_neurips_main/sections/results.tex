\section{Results}
SessionKV-DR is treated as the principal measurement surface for ALC behavior. The benchmark decomposes delayed recall, overwrite, persistence, and capacity/interference, enabling targeted interpretation.

Full ALC improvements over baseline support the intended runtime-memory function in this setup. Competitive no-write slices are reported explicitly and suggest meaningful limits in write policy and interference control.

TinyQA is supportive but insufficient for broad benchmark conclusions.
